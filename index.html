      About Unengaged of the Day

      Unengaged of the Day is a prayer resource designed to help Christians pray specifically and strategically for unreached and unengaged people groups around the world.

      An unreached people group is a people group among which there is no indigenous community of believing Christians with adequate numbers and resources to evangelize this people group without outside assistance.

      An unengaged people group has no known active church planting effort underway.

      Data is sourced from Joshua Project and other missions research organizations.
